About The Developers Behind Dovetail Chess

Dovetail Chess is a project by two high school sophomores, in which we have designed a game where you can play chess against an AI. We have also previously designed an AI that can give you the best possible move in your position.

Meet the AI Behind the Scenes

The AI behind the scenes goes by the name Dovetail. Dovetail is based off of pinging an LLM via Openrouter.
