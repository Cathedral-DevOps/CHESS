About The Developers Behind ChessBros

ChessBros is a project by two high school sophomores, in which we have designed a game where you can play chess against an AI or your friend. We have also designed an AI that can give you the best possible move in your position (Toggled on by default).

Meet the AI Behind the Scenes

The AI behind the scenes goes by two names that we have given him, ChessMax and Bob, as of writing this, he is currently being trained and having his base model code reverse-engineered as we had previously vibe-coded him.
